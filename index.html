About Me


My name is Adam. I currently reside in Indianapolis, IN. I graduated from Purdue University (Fort Wayne) in 2012 with a degree in Organizational Leadership and Supervision. Since that time I have held various service roles in business-to-business organizations. I have experience in building relationships, helping clients implament best practices, troubleshoot technical issues, and leading teams.

Problem solving has been at the core of all of my roles. In my previous roles I have needed to understand the business of my clients in order to help them get the most out of the software and services I supported. In my current role I am faced with more technical problems. I am tasked with finding the root cause, frequency, and revenue impact so development resources can be prioritized appropriately. This has required me to learn SQL to ensure I gather the necessary facts. These roles have inspired me to learn how to code as I view developers as the ultimate problem solvers.

In 2020, I made the decision to focus on learning how to code and transition to a career as a developer. I began the Web Development course through the Eleven Fifty Academy in May, 2020.
